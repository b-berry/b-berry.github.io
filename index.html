About

I'm a passionate Open-Source engineer who enjoys troubleshooting systems from Linux clusters to vintage motorcycles and sailboats. Born in the 'Discover the Spirit' lands of North Dakota and self-taught Linux administration on Fedora systems while a student at the University of Minnesota's Department of Mathematics, I've since migrated to New York City to start a career in Linux Engineering and I am currently a DevOps and Support Engineer for Debian based clusters on the Liquid Galaxy project - a Google initiated distributed immersive VR platform.

I've been 100% remote for the last two years of my tenure on this project and while not coding, you'll find me riding old motorcycles, and sailboats.
